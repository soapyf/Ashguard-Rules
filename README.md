# Rules

> [!NOTE]
> We may ask you to stop using something not covered below until it can be reviewed. Exceptions to rules can be made if all parties involved agree.

---

# Banned Equipment

- All SAC weapons
- Chaos Decretum Exodus Rifle

---

# General Rules

- Do not enter red spawn zones and do not shoot into them unless returning fire
- All vehicles and armor systems must be LBA compatible
- Avatars must be at least 1.5m tall to participate in combat
- Prejump must be enabled
- No loud or obnoxious sounds

---

# Weapons

- No infinite ammo weapons (this includes deployables)
- No weapons designed to kill through obstacles that would typically stop them
- Fully automatic raycast weapons are not allowed at this time
- Burst fire raycast is allowed if it has no 'haze'
- Shotguns must fire prim bullets or use raycast
- Bullets must not go through walls, barricades, or any shield implement designed to stop them

## Explosives & AOE

- Explosives must adhere to a 5m kill / 10m wound radius unless highly directional
- Explosives must not exceed these limits unless there is a wind-up time choreographed both visually and audibly for several seconds
- Explosives that exceed a 2m kill radius must be interceptable
- Explosive projectiles, missiles, or bombs that use non-physical movement or any other method designed to defeat interceptors are not allowed
- Explosives must not kill through walls or other solid objects
- Deployable mines must be at minimum 0.25 x 0.25 x 0.04 meters in size
- AoE/Explosive damage must not be group safe

---

# Movement Abilities

### Jetpacks

- Cannot recharge in air
- Minimum 3 second recharge time
- Should only move the avatar a reasonable distance (the full width of a region is not considered reasonable)

### Sprints

- Max 5 second duration
- Minimum 6 second recharge

### Diverolls & Dashes

- 15m max distance
- Cannot dash in air
- Minimum 4 second recharge

---

# Avatar Equipment

- Riot shields are allowed, but must not exceed 50 HP and must be directional
- Equipment that blinds and/or limits an avatar's movement must have a recharge time longer than the duration of its effect
- Body armor that protects a full 360° cannot exceed a 25% damage reduction at this time
- Healing abilities must have either limited uses per life or extended recharge times

---

# Deployables

- Deployable explosives must not have HP that exceeds 1 and must be destroyable by standard projectiles
- Max HP of 100 at deployment; can be repaired/constructed to higher values
- HP cannot exceed 400

### Auto-Turrets

- 1 turret per person
- Must be constructed and cannot be instantly functional upon deployment
- Maximum field of view/fire of 180 degrees
- Explosive ammo is only allowed on turrets with lock-on times

---

# Vehicles

All armored units must:
- Utilise LBA compatible armor
- Hover/jetpack no more than 15 meters above the ground
- Have a cooldown of at least 1 minute on any self-repair/healing functions
